Chuka University Nutrition and Dietetic Student Association

THE CLUB



Chuka University Nutrition and Dietetics Student Association (CUNDSA) is a club which was formed on the year 2022 by the Nutrition class of 2021.The main aim of the club is to bring nutrition students together and promote welfare,social relations and act as a link to other institutions offering the same course

our vision

To be an authority and resourse for promoting good nutrition

our mission

empowering members to be leaders in the practice of nutrition and dietetics in promotion of the nutritional well-being and health of the population.

objectives

  • To bring the nutriton students together
  • to promote education of individuals on nutrition concerns common to the university fraternity and their prevention,through activities such as medical camps and other health awareness events.
  • To promote social relations,social intergration and understanding of social diversity
  • To organize Nutrition forums,community projects and other association programs as regulated by the association constitution
  • To promote the welfare of members
  • Educate university students,community and entire population on the significance of good nutrition in relation to health,lifestyle and diseases.

More Feature

  • Bursary implementations
  • Association Assets
  • Nutrition seminars
  • Community Based projects
